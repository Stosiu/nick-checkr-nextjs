## Before you commit to a name

A few practical things worth considering before you register anything.

First, check availability across platforms. A name that's open on Instagram might already be taken on [Twitter](/check/twitter), [Reddit](/check/reddit), or [Twitch](/check/twitch). If you care about consistency, run the name through a multi-platform checker before you lock it in.

Second, understand the difference between [usernames and display names](/blog/username-vs-display-name). Your username is the permanent handle (or at least harder to change). Your display name is flexible. Pick the aesthetic username for your handle and you can always adjust the display name later.

Third, if your first choice is taken, don't just add numbers to the end. That defeats the purpose. Try combining words differently, swap in a synonym, or check whether the [username is actually taken or just inactive](/blog/username-already-taken).
